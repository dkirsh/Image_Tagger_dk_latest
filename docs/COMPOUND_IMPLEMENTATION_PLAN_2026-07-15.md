## Two build-time cautions carried from the skeptics (apply to every row)

1. **Keep each social field as its own output — do NOT sum it back into the general valence/hedonics score.**
   Many compounds reuse base measures (C1 integration, prospect, warm/cool ratio) that already feed the
   aggregate; adding the compound back double-weights the shared signal. (Flagged on C01, C04, C17, C14.)
2. **VGA/isovist collinearity.** C1 is computed from the visibility graph, so multiplying it by an isovist
   term risks squaring visibility. Where a compound crosses them (C17), restrict the isovist term to the
   directional isovist toward occupiable planes so it stays orthogonal to global integration.
