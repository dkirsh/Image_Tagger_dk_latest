# COMPOUND ATTRIBUTES FOR THE COGNITIVE + WELL-BEING CODES
## Report of the Expert Panel on Compound Attributes, 2026-07-15

*Commissioned by David Kirsh. The ask, precisely: not new PRIMITIVES (the prior committee did those) but
COMPOUND attributes — computable functions of two or more base measures whose value comes from their
INTERACTION, revealing a salient human effect (some improve outcomes, some worsen them), with special weight
on predicting WHICH FORM OF SOCIAL INTERACTION is more probable at region A vs region B of a space. Grounded
in two source docs read for this run: `POE_Cognitive_Code_v1_20260709.md` (physical→cognitive code, the 38→8
collapse) and `CNfA_Mechanisms_Measures_WellBeing_v2.md` (the synergy / antagonism / cascade / threshold /
masking / agency-buffer patterns that are the mechanism of every compound below, plus the
subjective→biosignal→physical measure triplet).*

**Method.** 12 expert seats proposed compounds grounded in their own literatures; a secretary merged 30
near-duplicate writeups across seats and clustered survivors by human effect; 8 adversarial skeptics
(checker≠author) then judged each on ONE decisive test above all — *is it really compound, or a disguised
average?* — plus computability, double-counting against the existing score layer, and evidence reality.
**68 raw proposals → 48 merged candidates → 46 survivors** (2 fully VIABLE at GREEN-adjacent, 32 VIABLE at an
AMBER ceiling, 12 needing a declared extra input) and **2 KILLED**. 21 agents; full machine-readable output
in `compound_result.json` beside this file.

**Why almost everything is AMBER, and that's correct.** A compound attribute inherits the weakest tier of its
inputs. Nearly all of these ride the Tier-B inferred plan grid (VGA integration, path overlap, isovist) or an
acoustic/material proxy, so AMBER is the honest ceiling — exactly as the prior committee found. Fabrication is
still the cardinal sin: 12 candidates ABSTAIN without a real seat map, occupancy, acoustic params, or a
declared 3D model, rather than inventing those inputs.

**The seats:** Whyte–Gehl triangulation · Hall–Sommer proxemics · Hillier–Sailer space syntax ·
acoustic-conversation ecology (Hongisto/ISO 3382-3) · Appleton prospect–refuge/safety · Whyte desire-lines ·
Ulrich–Kaplan biophilia-for-sociality · Chatterjee–Vartanian approach neuroaesthetics · Reber–Taylor
fluency-dwell · the CNfA masking methodologist · Schweiker multisensory non-additivity · hard-nosed workplace POE.

## 1. The organising idea, in one paragraph

The physical code measures the container; the cognitive code measures the building–occupant relation; and the
well-being register reads every measure as a COST or a RESOURCE. The reason to compound is that **the
interaction is the signal**: a shared anchor and a busy path each mean little alone, but an anchor ON the path
ignites conversation (synergy); big glass is a daylight RESOURCE that becomes a glare+reverberation COST
unless treated (antagonism); warm timber lifts mood while hard planes quietly load listening effort (masking).
A single primitive is blind to all three. Every compound below names its base measures, the explicit
interaction form (product / threshold-gate / ratio / inverted-U / sign-difference / super-additive), the human
effect and its SIGN, and — where social — the region-A-vs-B prediction it sharpens.

### C01 — `triangulation_ignition_field`  (conversation-initiation, rank 9, B/AMBER)

**Human effect (Raises t).** Probability that co-present strangers convert silent proximity into a triggered exchange because a salient shared anchor sits ON the high-integration desire line, giving each a permissible reason to speak.

**Social form:** triggered conversation / chance encounter

**Base measures compounded:** landmark salience, C1 visual integration (VGA), C6 path overlap, prospect (view depth), social.sociopetal, dwell_propensity

**Interaction (why it's more than its parts):** Threshold-gated spatial product: anchor_salience x integration/path-overlap, gated to ~0 unless the anchor's plan location falls within a proximity band of the max-integration desire-line ridge. The signal is strictly the CO-LOCATION, not either term.

**Computation.** Locate anchor centroids + salience s; sample integration I along a d-metre buffer of the desire-line ridge; gate g decaying with normal-distance offset; field = s*I*g per region. ABSTAINS where plan inference UNKNOWN or no anchor detected.

**Region A vs B.** Fountain on the diagonal cross-path between two entrances (ignites talk) vs identical fountain in a planted dead alcove off all circulation (admired, no talk).

**Evidence.** Whyte 1980: triangulation requires the prop to lie where people already cross; Hillier et al. 1993: natural movement — integration predicts pedestrian flow

**Skeptic — VIABLE.** Flagship of David's core social lever and the cleanest compound in the set. Computable NOW at Tier B. Chief residual risk is cross-tier registration: you must place a photo-detected salient anchor onto a single-photo-INFERRED plan grid and overlay it on a VGA ridge derived from that same inferred plan — a stack of heuristics, correctly capped at AMBER. Region-A-vs-B (cross-path fountain ignites vs dead-alcove fountain admired) follows directly from the gate. I would defend this to Kirsh.

*Really compound?* Yes — genuinely compound. The value comes strictly from CO-LOCATION: a threshold gate on the anchor's normal distance to the max-integration desire-line ridge. Salience alone or integration alone cannot fire it, and a plain average of salience and integration would wrongly score the dead-alcove fountain highly; the gate correctly zeros it. The interaction is real, not a disguised sum.

*Double-counting:* landmark salience and C1 integration also feed the hedonics/plan-score layer; if this field is ALSO summed into the aggregate score, both signals get re-weighted. Keep it as its own social field, not added back into the general valence score.

*Fix:* Require the anchor to be detected in photo-salience AND registered to a plan cell above a confidence threshold; abstain (UNKNOWN) when cross-tier registration confidence is low rather than guessing the centroid.


### C17 — `co_awareness_integration`  (chance-encounter, rank 8.5, A+B/AMBER)

**Human effect (Improves).** Whether being configurationally central actually translates into mutual awareness of other occupants — the precondition for a place feeling alive and for someone joining a group. Integration without sightlines to occupied space yields central-but-blind spots.

**Social form:** triggered conversation / sustained-huddle joining / chance encounter

**Base measures compounded:** C1 visual integration, prospect (view depth)

**Interaction (why it's more than its parts):** Product (not average): centrality (C1) x realized visibility of occupiable space (isovist from prospect). High C1 with low prospect (central but enclosed pocket) reads central on any single primitive but scores low here.

**Computation.** I=normalized C1; V=isovist area weighted toward directions containing occupiable planes; co_awareness=I*V, minus a small pure-ceiling/floor term.

**Region A vs B.** Integrated open-floor desk with long sightlines to the team (high) vs equally integrated desk behind a partition on the main route (passed by many, sees no one).

**Evidence.** Backhouse & Drew 1992: interaction tracks mutual visibility, not proximity; Rashid et al. 2006; Benedikt 1979 (isovist)

**Skeptic — VIABLE_CEILING_AMBER.** Clean central-but-blind mask with strong evidence and clear A-vs-B (integrated open desk vs integrated desk behind a partition). Downgraded from its self-assigned top rank because of the C1/isovist collinearity: the interaction is real only for the global-centrality-vs-local-directional-visibility distinction, which must be enforced in code or it degenerates into visibility-squared. AMBER.

*Really compound?* Marginally, and only under one reading. The claimed mask (high centrality, low realized visibility) requires C1 and prospect/isovist to be SEPARABLE. But VGA integration (C1) is itself computed from the visibility graph, so C1 and isovist prospect are largely the same underlying signal; multiplying them risks squaring visibility rather than capturing an interaction. It survives only because global configurational centrality (graph position across the whole plan) genuinely differs from the LOCAL directional isovist toward occupiable seats - a desk connected around a corner can be graph-central yet have a low forward isovist to occupied planes.

*Double-counting:* REAL and must be flagged: C1 (VGA) and prospect are both visibility-graph-derived and both feed the score/hedonics layer; compounding them re-weights visibility twice. Mitigate by restricting the V term strictly to the DIRECTIONAL isovist toward occupiable planes (not omnidirectional openness), so it is orthogonal to global integration.

*Fix:* value = C1_global * V_directional_to_occupiable, where V is explicitly the forward isovist restricted to occupiable planes, NOT total openness, to avoid squaring the visibility signal.


### C04 — `huddle_shelter_viability`  (confidential-huddle, rank 8.5, extra_input/AMBER)

**Human effect (Raises s).** Viability of a location for a formed pair/small group to hold a sustained or confidential exchange unseen and unheard by others; the deliberate inverse of the waiting/interaction seat.

**Social form:** sustained huddle / confidential exchange

**Base measures compounded:** C7 speech privacy (STI to others), C11 prospect-refuge / enclosure, C1 visual integration, acoustic absorption proxy, mean/variance depth, dwell_propensity

**Interaction (why it's more than its parts):** Product of seclusion complements: refuge x (1 - STI_to_others) x (1 - integration/exposure) x absorption. Any single high term (central location, acoustic leak) drives it toward zero; masking cross-check when visual closeness reads intimate but shelter is low.

**Computation.** R=refuge; Priv=1-STI_to_others; Seg=1-normalized C1; value=R*Priv*Seg*absorption. ABSTAINS on the acoustic term without a material/absorption table or declared 3D model (SCORED-partial geometry vs UNKNOWN privacy).

**Region A vs B.** Recessed backed absorptive alcove off the route (viable) vs open central node / glass-partition seat on a desire line (fails, masking flag).

**Evidence.** Sailer & McCulloh 2012: segregated locations host longer uninterrupted interaction; Osmond 1957 / Sommer 1969; Appleton 1975; ISO 3382-3; Alexander et al. 1977: alcoves / intimacy gradient

**Skeptic — NEEDS_EXTRA_INPUT.** The candidate itself declares tier=extra_input and is right to: the STI_to_others privacy term needs a material/absorption table or a declared 3D model and abstains from a bare photo. The geometry sub-part (refuge x segregation) is computable as SCORED-partial, but full value — the whole point, acoustic seclusion — requires the extra acoustic input. Honest design (partial-geometry SCORED vs privacy UNKNOWN). NEEDS_EXTRA_INPUT.

*Really compound?* Yes — product of complements: refuge x (1-STI_to_others) x (1-integration/exposure) x absorption. Conjunctive: any single high-exposure term drives it to zero, so it is a true multiplicative interaction (all seclusion conditions must co-hold), not an average of privacy-ish scores.

*Double-counting:* refuge/enclosure, integration, and absorption are shared with C05/C06 and it is the geometric mirror of C01/C06, so the plan-integration signal recurs across the family. Manage shared-input weighting at the score layer.


### C03 — `conversation_initiation_propensity`  (conversation-initiation, rank 8.5, A+B/AMBER)

**Human effect (Raises t).** Probability mere co-presence at a seat cluster converts into a triggered spoken exchange, driven by sociopetal (facing-in) orientation at conversational distance with low barriers.

**Social form:** triggered conversation

**Base measures compounded:** social.sociopetal, mean/variance depth (Hall band), enclosure index (barrier), C1 visual integration, C3 intelligibility

**Interaction (why it's more than its parts):** Sociopetal gate x Hall-distance bump (peak in social band ~1.2-3.6 m) x (1-barrier_opacity) x inverted-U on intelligibility. Sociofugal orientation vetoes the product regardless of the other terms.

**Computation.** Estimate seat facing vectors, pairwise facing-in fraction; bin centroid distances to Hall zones; barrier opacity along sightline; inverted-U on C3. ABSTAINS without recoverable seat orientation.

**Region A vs B.** Sociopetal chairs around a low table at social distance (high) vs parallel outward rows at the same footfall (initiation collapses).

**Evidence.** Osmond 1957: sociopetal vs sociofugal; Sommer & Ross 1958: facing-in ~doubled conversation; Hall 1966; Mehrabian & Diamond 1971

**Skeptic — NEEDS_EXTRA_INPUT.** The blocker is input, not logic. It requires recoverable seat FACING vectors; social.sociopetal sits in the cataloged-but-uncomputed registry, and chair orientation/pose from a single photo needs semantics classical CV does not reliably provide. The candidate honestly abstains without seat orientation, which means in current practice it abstains most of the time. Genuinely valuable once a seat/orientation map exists — hence NEEDS_EXTRA_INPUT rather than KILL.

*Really compound?* Yes, and the strongest interaction design in the set: the sociofugal veto multiplicatively zeros the whole product regardless of Hall-distance, barrier, or intelligibility. Combined with the Hall-band bump and an inverted-U on C3, it is emphatically non-additive — exactly the kind of interaction the brief wants.

*Double-counting:* C1 integration and C3 intelligibility are reused from the plan-metric layer; low concern relative to the input gap.


### C23 — `felt_safety_field`  (felt-safety-lingering, rank 8.5, A+B/AMBER)

**Human effect (Improves).** The below-awareness felt-safety FLOOR that gates whether a person will approach, stop, and stay in a region at all — the precondition for any lingering or social contact.

**Social form:** solitary retreat / fleeting co-presence (enabling floor beneath every form)

**Base measures compounded:** prospect (view depth), enclosure index, brightness variance, C1 visual integration

**Interaction (why it's more than its parts):** Threshold-gated MINIMUM (Liebig weakest-link): FSF=min(prospect, refuge_effective, escape, lighting_floor). A single deficient factor (unlit corner, blocked exit) collapses the field even if the other three are excellent.

**Computation.** R_eff = enclosure as inverted-U (entrapment penalty at full enclosure); E from C1/decision-point preview; L=min local luminance in lower periphery; FSF=min(P,R_eff,E,L). ABSTAINS for E without inferred plan.

**Region A vs B.** Glazed lobby edge with a lit backed alcove and visible exit (high) vs bright open center whose one recessed corner is unlit and exit occluded (FSF collapses on the min).

**Evidence.** Fisher & Nasar 1992 (concealment+prospect+escape combine); Blobaum & Hunecke 2005; Boomsma & Steg 2014; Appleton 1975

**Skeptic — VIABLE_CEILING_AMBER.** Correct non-additive form, correct sign (a low value suppresses dwell/approach for any occupant), and it is honestly framed as the enabling FLOOR beneath every social form. Minor spec nit: the named base measure 'brightness variance' is global while the computation uses min local luminance in the lower periphery — reconcile the named input with the lighting-floor term. ABSTAINS on escape without inferred plan → AMBER. rank 8.5 justified; best of the batch.

*Really compound?* Yes, and the form is correct: a threshold-gated MINIMUM (Liebig weakest-link) is exactly right for fear cues, which combine as a binding constraint, not a sum. One unlit corner or blocked exit collapses the field regardless of the other factors — an average would wrongly let a bright open plan compensate for a blocked exit.

*Double-counting:* The min form structurally resists double-weighting (it returns the binding factor, not a sum), so even though enclosure/prospect also feed other layers, no signal is silently doubled here. Low risk.


### C19 — `collaboration_bump_field`  (chance-encounter, rank 8, B/AMBER)

**Human effect (Improves).** Where colleagues' routine paths to shared destinations overlap AND they are within communication range — Kabo's bump space — which, not raw proximity, predicts new weak-tie formation and unplanned work-relevant exchange.

**Social form:** chance encounter -> triggered conversation (weak-tie formation)

**Base measures compounded:** C6 path overlap, C5 collaborator proximity, C23 social connectedness (commons gravity)

**Interaction (why it's more than its parts):** Super-additive product: path_overlap x within-range(Allen curve) x amenity_pull. Overlap without range = anonymous passing; proximity without shared paths = silent neighbours; only the conjunction generates repeated range-close crossings.

**Computation.** O=route-overlap density; Rng=distance decay per Allen curve (steep <8-15 m); Pull=amenity gravity; bump=O*Rng*Pull. ABSTAINS on C5 without seat/team map.

**Region A vs B.** Shared corridor where two teams' routes to a common coffee point overlap within ~10 m (weak ties) vs equal-draw coffee point reached by non-overlapping single-team approaches (few new ties).

**Evidence.** Kabo et al. 2014: overlapping bump spaces predict new collaborations beyond proximity; Allen 1977 (Allen curve); Sailer & McCulloh 2012

**Skeptic — VIABLE_CEILING_AMBER.** Strong, well-evidenced compound. The Allen-curve range gate on path overlap is exactly the interaction Kabo isolates. Region A-vs-B (overlapping ~10 m corridor to shared coffee vs non-overlapping single-team approaches) follows directly from the computation. Correctly ABSTAINS on C5 without a seat/team map, which is what forces the AMBER ceiling. Defensible to Kirsh.

*Really compound?* Yes — genuine super-additive product O*Rng*Pull. Overlap without range = anonymous passing; proximity without shared paths = silent neighbours; only the conjunction yields repeated range-close crossings. The value lives in the interaction, not an average.

*Double-counting:* Pull term is C23 commons-gravity used as one input; not re-weighted elsewhere in this attribute. Low risk. Watch that C6 path-overlap and C5 proximity are computed on the SAME inferred plan so the product isn't two views of one geometry.


### C13 — `self_congestion_conversation_pinch`  (conversation-initiation, rank 8, B/AMBER)

**Human effect (Inverted).** The counter-intuitive Whyte hotspot where the highest share of people-in-conversation occurs — the busiest crossing/entry pinch where high through-movement plus a mild narrowing plus mutual visibility make people stop and talk mid-stream.

**Social form:** chance encounter / triggered conversation embedded in the moving stream

**Base measures compounded:** C6 path overlap, threshold density, C12 crowding risk

**Interaction (why it's more than its parts):** Threshold-gated non-monotone composition: path_overlap x pinch_narrowing x visibility, multiplied by an inverted-U crowding penalty that rises then falls at the comfort knee. Neither raw flow nor raw pinch alone predicts the peak.

**Computation.** base=F*P*V; m=crowd*(1-crowd)/0.25 (peaks at moderate crowding); value=base*m. Rides inferred plan -> AMBER; ABSTAINS if movement grid UNKNOWN.

**Region A vs B.** Doorway/corridor-merge pinch (people plant and talk mid-stream) vs wide even mid-corridor of equal traffic (pure pass-through).

**Evidence.** Whyte 1980: self-congestion — most conversation in the center of pedestrian flow; Hillier et al. 1993; Gehl 1987/2011

**Skeptic — VIABLE_CEILING_AMBER.** Best-evidenced, most genuinely non-monotone interaction here; Tier-B computable on the inferred plan; high A-vs-B value (pinch vs even corridor follows from the P term). ## 6. How these plug into the region-A-vs-B social engine (CW synthesis)

The panel's centre of gravity is exactly David's goal: a small family of **social-form fields**, each a
compound whose A-vs-B contrast names which interaction a region affords.

- **Conversation-initiation** — `triangulation_ignition_field` (C01, the flagship: anchor × integration gated
  by co-location on the desire line), `triangulated_conversation_permission` (C02, same, gated by acoustic
  hearability), `conversation_initiation_propensity` (C03, sociopetal × distance-band × barrier).
- **Confidential huddle** (opposite optimum) — `huddle_shelter_viability` (C04): refuge × (1−STI-to-others) ×
  (1−exposure) × absorption. The SAME acoustic-privacy term that protects a huddle KILLS group audibility —
  the panel's cleanest antagonism, and the reason one foyer must be scored region by region.
- **Chance encounter** — `co_awareness_integration` (C17, the "central-but-blind" mask: global centrality ×
  directional isovist to *occupiable* planes, deliberately not omnidirectional openness, to avoid squaring
  visibility).
- **Felt-safety / lingering** — the precondition fields (prospect × refuge × exit-visibility × corner-lighting);
  the lone-waiter and the huddle read the SAME field with opposite sign.
- **Masking-diagnostics** (11 of them — the largest cluster) — compounds whose value IS an antagonism:
  `warm_look_reverberant_room_divergence` (C05) fires when positive affect cues co-occur with an uncorrected
  acoustic cost; these are "measure this with the triplet before believing the room" flags, not comfort scores.

**Two engineering cautions the skeptics kept raising, to honour before building any of these:**
1. **Double-counting.** Many compounds reuse base measures (C1 integration, prospect, warm/cool ratio) that
   already feed the hedonics/aggregate score. Keep each social field as its OWN output; do NOT sum it back into
   the general valence score, or the shared signal gets re-weighted. (Flagged explicitly on C01, C04, C17, C43.)
2. **VGA/isovist collinearity.** C1 is computed from the visibility graph, so multiplying it by an isovist term
   risks squaring visibility. Where a compound crosses the two (C17), restrict the isovist term to the
   DIRECTIONAL forward isovist toward occupiable planes, keeping it orthogonal to global integration.

**Recommended first build (CW, not the panel):** C01 `triangulation_ignition_field` — it is the single cleanest
expression of the whole social thesis, computable at Tier B now, with the sharpest A-vs-B demonstration
(cross-path fountain ignites vs dead-alcove fountain admired). Ship it as one socket predicate (registry entry
with `requires`={plan, anchor-detection}, derivation chokepoint, M1 replay, abstain-on-low-registration), then
the huddle/permission pair (C04/C02) so the antagonism is demonstrable on one image, then the masking-diagnostic
cluster as a distinct "trust flag" channel. Each faces the correctness panel like C1–C24 did; literature anchors
go through Article_Eater grounding before any candidate ships.
